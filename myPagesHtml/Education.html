MY EDUCATIONAL JOURNEY

Nursery & Primary School

When i was very young, aged 4 i started my educational journey at Longman Memorial Kindergaten School, located at Brikama. I spent 3 years in that level. Then i continued in the same school my Primary level from grade 1 to 6. That was from 2008 to 2014. I was among on the best students at the time which made me realised some of my potentials, as a result laid a foundation and i became dedicated to pursue for higher heights.

Junior School

Upon completing the primary level, in 2015, I sat for an entrance exams to continue my school carrier which i became successful in the exams. Then in 2015 i was enrolled at St.Peter's Technical Junior School and i was in the best class which really motivated me to work even harder due to the fact that when your grades drop, you have to shift to another class. Then in 2018 i sat for GABECE exams and i came out with a flying color with aggregate 10. This was from 2015 to 2018.

Senior School

Then in 2019, i continue pursuing my educational journey in the same school which is St.Peter's Technical Senior High School. I became a Science student and continue working hard then in 2021, i did WAEC exams where i came out with an outstanding result. This opened doors of scholarship applications for me to pursue my dreams in the university level. My senior level was from 2019 to 2021. Then i graduated from senior High.

University

After graduating from Senior School, i applied a scholarship under our government to study in Morocco, then i was given a scholarship to continue my educational carrier. In 2022, i was enrolled in Ecole Nationale des Sciences Applique de Fes (ENSAF), where i did my 2 years studying general Engineering, Then in my 3rd year i specialized in Embedded Systems and AI engineering. This is a Master's program where i will continue upto 2027 In Shaa Allah.

LONGTERM VISION
